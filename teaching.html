My Teaching Philosophy

I believe that everyone can learn to code and understand technology. My approach combines:

  • Hands-on learning - Learning by doing, not just reading
  • Real-world applications - Connecting theory to practical problems
  • Growth mindset - Embracing challenges and learning from mistakes
  • Community building - Creating supportive learning environments
